Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Everything you need to know about our events

General Questions

How do I find events on Eventify?

You can easily find events by browsing through our categories, such as seminars, festivals, and competitions. Use the search bar to look for specific events or filter by date and location.

How can I register for an event?

To register for an event, simply visit the event page and click on the "Register" or "Tickets" button. Follow the instructions to complete your registration or purchase.

What types of events does the university host?

The university hosts a variety of events, including academic seminars, cultural festivals, workshops, competitions, career fairs, and alumni meetups.

Can I cancel or transfer my event registration?

Cancellation and transfer policies vary by event. Please check the event details for specific instructions. If allowed, cancellations or transfers can be done through your account.

What happens if I miss an event I registered for?

If you miss an event, please check with the event organizer for any possible rescheduling or alternative viewing options. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for missed events unless specified in the event's terms.

Will food and beverages be provided?

Some events offer complimentary refreshments, while others may have food vendors on-site. Check the event details for specific information.
